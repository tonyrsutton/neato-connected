## Supported ESP-32 Boards

Below is a list of supported ESP-32 boards and **confirmed** as working:

* ESP-32-WROOM-32 (also known as ESP-32-D0WD-V3) - branded as JZK ESP-32S from Amazon UK - £7.29 (as of 29th Dec 2026). Validated by [@tonyrsutton](https://github.com/tonyrsutton)
* ESP32-C3 QFN32 - branded as GERUI ESP32-C3 from Amazon FR. Validated by Meneldor (on our Discord channel)
